Round 2:
Threads of
Play

Acrylic on Canvas
50 x 60cm
2022

In this artwork, there are two girls from different cultural backgrounds which is one from Malaysia and the other from Japan. They share a moment of joy through a familiar game. While their clothes and origins are distinct, the way they toss and catch small objects shows how play transcends borders. This piece celebrates how traditional games like Batu Seremban and Otedama connect people beyond language, distance, and time.
