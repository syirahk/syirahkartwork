These are portrait drawings I made based on requests from my friends. All of them were hand-drawn with care, using the cross-hatching technique to add depth and texture. Each piece reflects a bit of their personality through my personal drawing style.

Caricature

These caricature drawings were made during a live school event at IOI Mall to promote Sekolah Seni. Most of the artworks were based on reference photos of Malaysian artists and public figures, drawn on the spot as examples for visitors.
