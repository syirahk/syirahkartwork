About Me

My name is Syirah, also known as Nik. I’m currently a Mass Communication (MC112) student with a deep love for drawing, design, and everything visual. I began drawing at the age of 4, but my creative journey took off at 10, when I started designing personalized keychains for classmates using materials from my father’s print shop. That little store. filled with merchandise tools and design supplies. It was where my passion for graphic design first bloomed.
My father, who also dabbles in design, became one of my early inspirations. By 16, I had begun experimenting with Adobe apps, and during my time at Sekolah Seni Malaysia Johor, I pursued a course in Visual Communication that sharpened my skills further especially in Adobe Illustrator.
Though I don’t box myself into a specific style, I enjoy creating pieces with emotional depth and intricate detail. Whether it’s a portrait or fanart of something I love, I draw what moves me. I’m also passionate about digital art involving OC (original characters), and I love collaborating with others to bring their characters to life.
For me, creativity has no strict boundaries. It just vibes, mood, and a lot of heart. Let’s build something cool together.

Exhibition

Lokaratna

The name "Lokaratna" is formed from two beautiful Sanskrit words “Loka”, meaning the world, and “Ratna”, meaning gems or precious stones.
Together, they paint a picture of a world filled with treasures. That is a metaphor for me as a students as emerging artists, each shaped and polished through years of creative exploration at Sekolah Seni Malaysia Johor.
Like radiant gems, they carry the spark of imagination and the promise to light up the landscape of Visual Arts in Malaysia
