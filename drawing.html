Drawing

A Thousand
Doves Gather

Mixed Media on Paper
42.0 x 59.4 cm
2023

This artwork portrays a flock of doves gathering together, symbolizing unity, companionship, and the instinct to move as one. Where there is one dove, many others will follow. Also, it is showing how togetherness brings strength. Much like humans, doves seek the comfort of a group, reminding us that unity is what helps us thrive.

Technique

Cross hatching

Directional lines

Blending
